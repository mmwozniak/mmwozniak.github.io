I am a cognitive neuroscientist. In my scientific work I am interested in social, psychological and neural mechanisms of self and identity (see My research for details). In my research I am especially interested in applying diverse methodological approaches from:

  • neuroscience (EEG, fMRI)
  • experimental psychology
  • data science & machine learning
  • robotics

Currently I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Social Cognition in Human-Robot Interaction Lab at Italian Institute of Technology in Genova, Italy where I investigate how operating a robot can influence our self-representation.
