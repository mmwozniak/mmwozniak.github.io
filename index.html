I am a cognitive neuroscientist. In my scientific work I am interested in a range of topics spanning cognitive neuroscience, social cognition, robotics, computational social science, and consciousness science (see My research for details). In my research I am especially interested in applying diverse methodological approaches from:

  • neuroscience (EEG, fMRI)
  • experimental psychology
  • data science & machine learning
  • robotics

Currently I am an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Applied Psychology at Jagiellonian University in Cracow, Poland and a senior Postdoctoral Researcher at Social Cognition in Human-Robot Interaction Lab at Italian Institute of Technology in Genova, Italy.
